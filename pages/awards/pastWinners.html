All Africa Music Awards (AFRIMA) is committed to promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion. We celebrate and honor the creativity and talent of African artists without discrimination based on race, color, age, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity/expression, national origin, or any other characteristic protected under applicable law. We believe in fostering an inclusive environment where all artists can thrive and contribute to the vibrant cultural heritage of Africa.
